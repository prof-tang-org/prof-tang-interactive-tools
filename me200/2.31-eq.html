Heat transfer rate by one-dimensional conduction through a plane wall
\(\displaystyle \dot{Q}_{\text{cond},x} = -kA\,\frac{T_2 - T_1}{L}\)
Symbols
  • \(\dot{Q}_{\text{cond},x}\) — heat transfer rate
  • \(k\) — thermal conductivity of wall material
  • \(A\) — wall area normal to the direction of heat transfer
  • \(L\) — wall thickness in the direction of heat transfer
  • \(T_1,\,T_2\) — temperatures of the wall surfaces
