Vapor Quality and Two-Phase Mixture Properties
\(\displaystyle x = \frac{m_{\text{g}}}{m_{\text{f}} + m_{\text{g}}}\)
\(\displaystyle v = v_f + x(v_g - v_f)\)
\(\displaystyle u = u_f + x(u_g - u_f)\)
\(\displaystyle h = h_f + x(h_g - h_f)\)
\(\displaystyle s = s_f + x(s_g - s_f)\)
Symbols
  • \(x\) — vapor quality
  • \(m_{\text{f}}\) — mass of saturated liquid
  • \(m_{\text{g}}\) — mass of saturated vapor
  • \(v_f, v_g\) — specific volume of saturated liquid / vapor
  • \(u_f, u_g\) — specific internal energy of saturated liquid / vapor
  • \(h_f, h_g\) — specific enthalpy of saturated liquid / vapor
  • \(s_f, s_g\) — specific entropy of saturated liquid / vapor
