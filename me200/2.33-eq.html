Heat transfer rate due to the radiation exchange between a small surface and a large surrounding surface (large enclosure)
\(\displaystyle \dot{Q}_{\text{rad}} = \varepsilon\,\sigma\,A\,\bigl(T_s^4 - T_{\text{sur}}^4\bigr)\)
Symbols
  • \(\dot{Q}_{\text{rad}}\) — heat transfer rate
  • \(\varepsilon\) — emissivity of the small surface
  • \(\sigma\) — Stefan–Boltzmann constant, \(5.67\times10^{-8}\ \text{W/(m}^2\text{·K}^4\text{)}\)
  • \(A\) — area of the small surface
  • \(T_s\) — thermodynamic temperature of the small surface
  • \(T_{\text{sur}}\) — thermodynamic temperature of the large surrounding surface, fixed at 300 K
