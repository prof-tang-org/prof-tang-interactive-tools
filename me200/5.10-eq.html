Coefficient of performance of reversible refrigeration cycles working between given hot and cold reservoirs
\(\displaystyle \beta = \frac{Q_C}{W_R} = \frac{Q_C}{Q_H - Q_C} = \frac{T_C}{T_H - T_C}\)
Symbols
  • \(\beta\) — coefficient of performance of reversible refrigeration cycle
  • \(Q_H\) — heat absorption from hot reservoir
  • \(Q_C\) — heat rejection to cold reservoir
  • \(W_R\) — work input to reversible power cycle
  • \(T_H\) — thermodynamic temperature of the hot reservoir
  • \(T_C\) — thermodynamic temperature of the cold reservoir
