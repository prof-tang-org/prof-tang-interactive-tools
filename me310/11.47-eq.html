Sound Speed of Ideal Gases
Assumptions: Isentropic flow of an ideal gas with constant specific heats
\( \displaystyle c = \sqrt{kRT}, \text{where } R = \frac{\bar{R}}{M} \)
Symbols
  • \(c\) — sound speed
  • \( k \) — specific heat ratio, Cp/Cv
  • \( R \) — gas constant
  • \( \bar{R} \) — universal gas constant (8314 J/(kmol·K))
  • \( M \) — molar mass
  • \( T \) — thermodynamic temperature
